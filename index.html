What is BNSC token?

How to buy BNSC token?
Follow these steps or join our Telegram group if you need help.
01.
Create a Trust Wallet
BNSC token is available on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Trust is the market leader in BSC (Binance Smart Chain) wallets. On Google Chrome, visit Trustwallet.com to download the extension and set up a wallet. On mobile? Get Trust Wallet's app for iPhone or Android.
02.
Send BNB to Trust Wallet
Buy BNB through Trust Wallet or transfer it to your Trust Wallet address from another wallet (e.g. Coinbase or Binance).
03.
Visit PancakeSwap
You can currently buy BNSC token on PancakeSwap, BNSC's official decentralized exchange where you can swap any ERC20 token.
04.
Swap BNB for BNSC
Enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap for BNSC. Click Connect Wallet then Swap.
